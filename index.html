Welcome to Humanitarians AI

Welcome to Humanitarians AI, a global collective of data scientists and creatives transforming artificial intelligence into a force for good. Explore how our ethical, innovative, and open-source AI solutions address real-world challenges in education, healthcare, nonprofits, and the arts.

Our Mission

At Humanitarians AI, we aim to advance ethical AI through mentorship, education, and collaboration. By fostering a culture of transparency, inclusion, and community, we ensure that AI serves humanity, addressing societal challenges while empowering individuals and organizations to use this technology responsibly.

AI of Good Projects

Discover our innovative projects that bring AI to life for the greater good. From educational tools like Project Dewey, which makes AI accessible to learners, to Project RAMAN Effect, which revolutionizes public health through wastewater-based epidemiology, our initiatives bridge the gap between cutting-edge AI research and societal impact.

Fellows & Mentors

Join a community dedicated to advancing ethical AI. Our Fellows Program bridges the gap between graduation and impactful employment, offering hands-on learning and mentorship from industry leaders. Mentors play a pivotal role in guiding Fellows, fostering innovation, and ensuring the ethical development of AI solutions.
