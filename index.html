About Nik Bear Brown

Nik Bear Brown is an AI educator and consultant with a passion for leveraging AI in education, creative expression, and ethical innovation. As the AI Lead for Northeastern University’s AI for Education initiative (AI4ED) and a seasoned teaching professor, Nik specializes in generative AI, prompt engineering, and experiential learning. His work bridges academic research with practical applications to transform education and empower organizations globally.
