About Bear Brown and Company

Bear Brown and Company is a consulting firm founded by Nik Bear Brown, an expert in AI, machine learning, and data analytics. We specialize in innovation incubation, project acquisition, and strategic consultation to help organizations harness the power of AI and data to achieve their goals. With a commitment to impactful, ethical AI solutions, we work with businesses, nonprofits, and educators to drive innovation and success.

What Does Bear Brown Do?

  • Project Acquisition & Networking: Bringing in valuable projects and establishing networks to support Humanitarians' initiatives.
  • Innovation Incubation: Acting as an incubator for innovative ideas, helping foster new developments in AI and related fields.
  • Strategic Consultation: Offering specialized consulting services in AI, machine learning, and data analytics.

What Does Humanitarians AI Do?

Nik Bear Brown is the founder and director of Humanitarians AI, a nonprofit organization dedicated to experiential learning and continuous growth for individuals, particularly international students.

  • Hands-on Learning: Providing real-world project experience at no cost.
  • Mission: Enabling "Real Experiential Education" for practical, impactful learning.
  • Advocacy: Recognizing the significant role of international students in the U.S. workforce and championing their contributions.

Our Key Products Include:

  • Chatbots: Conversational AI for various applications.
  • Lyrical Literacy: Song generation technology to support creative literacy.
  • Image Generation: Tools for creating visuals for Kindle books, coloring books, and more.
