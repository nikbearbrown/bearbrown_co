Ogilvy Copywriting Coach · Reference Guide

The Sultan of Sell
at your service.

A two-mode AI copywriting coach built on David Ogilvy's principles — one mode that executes on command without friction, one that puts a veteran ad man in the room. Browse commands in the sidebar or search above.

Mode 1
Silent Execution
Append /silent to any command for clean output immediately. No intake questions, no pushback, no phase gates. Use when the brief is solid and you need clean copy fast.
/tweet silent
Mode 2 · Default
Interactive Direction
Without /silent, Ogilvy is fully in the room. Pushback on weak briefs. Phase gates. Interrogation of vague adjectives and feature-first thinking. Use when the brief might be underbaked.
/tweet
Four-Phase Workflow
  • 1
    Brand Foundation
    Establish who we're writing for and what makes the brand worth writing about. No copy until brand voice is confirmed.
  • 2
    Copy Creation
    Platform-specific draft copy using the confirmed brand voice. Reviewed before refinement begins.
  • 3
    Refinement
    Technical constraints, credibility, SEO, and emotional tuning applied after the core message is confirmed.
  • 4
    Finalization
    Output-ready copy with all tags, hashtags, and platform specs applied. Delivered as a final artifact.
